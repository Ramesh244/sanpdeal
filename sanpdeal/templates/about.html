About Snapdeal

Snapdeal is India's leading value e-commerce platform that has helped Indian e-commerce grow beyond just brands & urban users. Nearly 80% of Snapdeal's users come from non-metro locations highlighting its deep reach across the length and breadth of the country. Snapdeal hosts a wide selection of good quality, value-priced merchandise across fashion, home, beauty & personal care, general merchandise, and other categories. With more than 200 Mn app installations, it is one of India's top online shopping destinations.

Mission and Vision of Snapdeal

Vision

At Snapdeal, we aim to become India’s go-to shopping destination for value-conscious consumers-both online and offline. Snapdeal believes in transforming people’s lives and meet their aspirations through a sustainable ecosystem that is built on trust, respect, inclusion and conscientiousness. Positive Impact is at the core of Snapdeal’s strategy.

    Mission

    Snapdeal’s mission is to help Indian families unlock and achieve their aspirations by acquiring trendy and stylish products at affordable prices.

    Values and Principles of Snapdeal

    Always be Curious

    At Snapdeal, we ask questions and a lot of them. And we continue to do so till we get to the heart of the matter.

    Own Outcomes

    We recognize that while efforts are essential, they must bring quantable results.     Hence, we take complete ownership responsibility for the results and not just for the process.

    Do the Right Thing

    Every step that we take has to be in the interest of the organization.     While what is right will vary in different situations, we should use our judgment to determine the right action

    Be Respectful
